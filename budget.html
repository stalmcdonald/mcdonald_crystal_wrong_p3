Party on a Budget

Themes on a Budget

First decide on your Theme. Maybe it's a MadHatter Tea Party for Halloween, or High Tea in the garden during the summer, or a Traditional Annual Tea Party. No matter what the special occasion, it's good to have a theme.

Locations on a Budget

Find a venue location. If you're on a tight budget, then your house would be your best bet. If you have a nice garden in your backyard it can be an ideal area to have a tea party hosted economically. Other great low cost solutions for locations would be to try having it at a club or rent out a restaurant, community center, in your home or on a patio deck. Staying close to home, can save you a lot of money.

Creating a Guest List on a budget

Create your guest list. Decide how many people you would like to invite. A small budget should have no more than 5-15 guests. Remember, you will need to feed and entertain your guest. Keep the party small to avoid high food and entertainment costs.

Menus on a Budget

Create your menu in advance. Doing a potluck can save you a considerable amount of money. Ask friends to bake their best tea dish. Guests who do not feel comfortable cooking can bring finger foods created at the deli

Music on a Budget

There are free music sources that can meet your needs. For a list of of music resources click here. iTunes or your iPod with a great playlist can easily set the mood. If you are not confident in your music taste try asking a friend for an opinion or to DJ. Party games, Card Games, or a food demo from a great cook is a perfect way to stay with in budget!

Invitations on a Budget

Send out invitations. If you plan on saving money on a stamp and personally delivering it to them. There are also free online services such as www.evite.com for the environmentally conscience to send the invites electronically for free

Decorating on a Budget

Consider your decorations. A little can go a long way to set the mood for a party. A garden is visually appealing and offers its own decoration. Be creative and have fun. Enjoy entertaining! Being a great host makes the party successful. If your guests feel comfortable they can let loose and enjoy themselves.
